## 🚀 Problem Statement

Legal professionals, law students, and organizations spend significant time:

- Searching through lengthy judgments and case laws
- Analyzing contracts and legal documents
- Identifying important clauses and legal risks
- Drafting repetitive legal documents
- Finding relevant precedents efficiently

The growing volume of legal information makes legal research increasingly complex and time-consuming.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Nyaya-AI leverages Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide:

- Intelligent legal document analysis
- Context-aware precedent discovery
- Conversational legal assistance
- Automated legal drafting
- Faster legal research workflows

## 🔄 Workflow

1. User uploads a legal document or submits a legal query.
2. FastAPI backend processes the request.
3. NLP and AI models analyze the content.
4. Semantic search retrieves relevant precedents.
5. RAG pipeline generates contextual legal insights.
6. Results are displayed through the Nyaya Assistant interface.
7. Users can generate summaries, drafts, and recommendations.
