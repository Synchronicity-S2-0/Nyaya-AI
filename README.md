## 🚀 The Problem

Legal professionals, students, and organizations spend countless hours navigating lengthy judgments, contracts, and legal documents.

### Challenges

- ⏳ Time-consuming legal research
- 📚 Difficulty finding relevant precedents
- 📄 Manual document analysis
- ✍️ Repetitive legal drafting
- 🗂️ Information overload

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Nyaya-AI combines Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to create a unified legal intelligence platform.

### What Nyaya-AI Provides

- 📄 Smart document analysis
- 🔍 Semantic precedent discovery
- 💬 Conversational legal assistant
- ✍️ Automated legal drafting
- ⚖️ Structured legal reasoning

---

## ✨ Core Features

| Feature | Description |
|----------|------------|
| 📄 Document Synthesis | Summarize legal documents and identify risks |
| 🔍 Precedent Search | Semantic and vector-based case law retrieval |
| 💬 Nyaya Assistant | Conversational legal AI assistant |
| ✍️ Smarter Drafting | AI-assisted legal draft generation |
| ⚖️ Legal Reasoning | Context-aware legal insights |
